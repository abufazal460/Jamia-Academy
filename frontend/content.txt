About Jamia Academy

Jamia Academy, established in 2022, is a future-focused education group founded with the objective of expanding access to high-quality, technology-driven education aligned with global standards. Strategically located in close proximity to Jamia Millia Islamia University, the Academy benefits from a rich academic environment that reflects intellectual depth, research orientation, and a strong educational legacy.

The institution is led by Dr. Qasim Chaudhary, Founder & Director of Jamia Academy Group of Institutions, a distinguished academic leader, education strategist, and social-impact entrepreneur. Under his leadership, Jamia Academy has grown into a multi-branch education group operating academic institutions and a school, guided by a long-term vision of scalability, quality assurance, and inclusive growth.

A key strength of Jamia Academy lies in its academically strong and research-oriented faculty, which includes Ph.D. alumni of Jamia Millia Islamia University. This ensures that teaching methodologies are grounded in conceptual clarity, academic discipline, and outcome-based learning rather than short-term skill training alone.

Jamia Academy places strategic emphasis on Computer Science, Information Technology, and Creative Digital disciplines such as Graphics Design and Animation, aligning its academic offerings with global workforce trends and the evolving digital economy.

Since its establishment in 2022, Jamia Academy has remained committed to affordable and accessible education, particularly for students from economically weaker, marginalized, and underrepresented communities.

Beyond academic delivery, Jamia Academy views education as a catalyst for social mobility, national development, and economic resilience.

Guided by the vision “Empowering Minds. Advancing Technology. Building the Nation,” Jamia Academy aspires to emerge as a nationally and globally recognized education group.

Jamia Academy continues to evolve as more than an educational institution-it is a platform for knowledge, innovation, and inclusive progress.

Founder & CMD
Dr. Qasim Chaudhary
Dr. Qasim Chaudhary is a distinguished academic leader, education strategist, and social-impact entrepreneur with a strong commitment to building inclusive, technology-driven learning ecosystems. He holds a Doctorate (Ph.D.) and MBA from the University of Lucknow, and a BBA from Sherwood Group of Colleges, Nainital, combining academic research excellence with managerial and institutional leadership expertise.

As the Founder & Director of Jamia Academy Group of Institutions, Dr. Chaudhary oversees multiple academic branches and a school, guiding the organization with a long-term vision focused on scalability, quality assurance, and social impact.

In addition to institutional leadership, Dr. Chaudhary actively contributes to public education governance as a Member of the Uttar Pradesh Government School Management Committee.

Under his direction, Jamia Academy places strategic emphasis on Computer Science, Information Technology, and Creative Digital Disciplines such as Graphics Design.

Dr. Chaudhary envisions Jamia Academy as a national platform for skill development, innovation, and socio-economic mobility.

Our Mission
To provide high-quality education in Computer Science, Information Technology, and Creative Digital Fields that meets global standards and industry expectations.
To make affordable and accessible education available to students from economically weaker, marginalized, and underrepresented communities.
To expand Jamia Academy’s presence across multiple regions, ensuring consistent academic quality and scalable impact.
To integrate practical skills, ethical values, and technological competence into all academic programs.
